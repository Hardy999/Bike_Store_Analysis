## Business Recommendations 
- Focus growth efforts on Mountain and Road Bike categories.
- Replicate successful practices from Baldwin Bikes in lower-performing stores.
- Use top sales representatives as benchmarks for training and performance improvement.
- Explore targeted marketing or expansion strategies in Texas.
- Align inventory planning with consistently high-performing bike models.
